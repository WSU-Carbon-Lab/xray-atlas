# Polarization and molecular orientation

The transition intensity scales as the squared projection of the polarization
onto the transition dipole moment,
$I \propto |\hat{\mathbf{e}}\cdot\boldsymbol{\mu}_{if}|^{2}$, so spectra
collected at several incidence angles determine average orientation.
