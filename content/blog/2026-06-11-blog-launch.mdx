# Introducing the X-ray Atlas blog

We are launching the X-ray Atlas blog. It will be the primary place for
announcements of new features and bug fixes, other platform updates, and some
insight into the development philosophy and process behind them.

The stakeholders for this platform have spread beyond what we initially
envisioned. We now have interested parties from various synchrotron
facilities, beamline scientists, industry researchers, academic institutions,
and more, each with their own level of interest and involvement in the
platform. Because of this, we need to improve our ability to communicate with
these stakeholders at the level each group wants to engage at.

## Expectations and roadmap

You can expect a mix of technical posts, release announcements, perspective
pieces, and how-to guides, sorted into their own categories on the
[blog index](/blog). Think of these posts as extended patch notes detailing
what we are up to in the development of the project at any given time.

## RSS feed

If you would rather follow along in a feed reader, subscribe to the
[RSS feed](/blog/rss.xml) for new posts as they publish, or to a
[category-specific feed](/blog) if you only care about, say, releases.
